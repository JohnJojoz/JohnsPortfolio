STANDBY

× MY ROLE: WEB DESIGN - UX DESIGN

× TYPE OF PROJECT: GOLDSMITHS DEGREE WEBSITE

× LINK (AS OF 2024): www.goldsmithsmediashow.com/

Standby is a digital showcase of diverse creative work from Goldsmiths' Media and Communication students that I had created. Curated to capture the spirit of our time, it reflects the challenges and hopes of a generation. This website presents a range of media, from animation and photography to journalism and interactive design, united by a shared experience of uncertainty and solidarity. As we move forward, Standby offers a platform to celebrate our creativity and anticipate the future.
